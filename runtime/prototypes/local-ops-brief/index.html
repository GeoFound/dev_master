Local Ops Brief

Audience: Solo operator managing multiple AI-built projects

Summarize active runs, blocked decisions, and evidence status in one local prototype screen.

Problem

The operator needs a quick way to see what happened overnight without reading raw logs.

Workflow

  1. Scan overnight run status
  2. Open blocked review items
  3. Check evidence freshness
  4. Decide whether to continue, hold, or escalate

Success Signal

The prototype loads locally and shows the core workflow without external services.
